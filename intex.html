How can you book journey flight tickets?

Here are easy steps to book Journey flight tickets.

  1. First, go to the goJourney.in website or open the journey mobile app.
  2. Then, visit the booking page and enter the ‘to’ and ‘from’ destinations.
  3. Choose the date of travel and number of passengers.
  4. In case you are eligible for a special fare, please select from – 6Exclusive, Armed Forces, Student, Senior Citizen, LTC, Family & Friends and Unaccompanied Minor.
  5. Click on the ‘Search Flight’ button to receive a list of options along with the flight ticket prices. Choose the flight as per your preference.
  6. Confirm your booking after entering all the required details and get ready to enjoy on-time, hassle-free and courteous service on board.

Note: While making the payment for your flight tickets, do check out the various cashback and other deals available on the offers page. This will enable you to enjoy additional savings on your booking.
